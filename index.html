Frequently Asked Questions

Do you admit children of all religions?

In spite of the fact that this is a Christian Based School, we admit pupils of all religions.

Won't my children be discriminated if they are not Catholics?

Not all all! We respect all doctrines and give time to pupils of different religions to worship their God.

Does you school teach followig the curriculum?

We follow teaching the guidelines provided by the Ministry of Education and Sports (MOES) together with the National Curriculum Development Centre (NCDC).

Do you provide accomodation to pupils who live far away from your school?

We have dormitories and well trained matrons and wardens who take care of the boarding pupils.

Will your school develop my child's talents?

Our school conducts co-curricular activities which enable learners to develop and discover their talents.
